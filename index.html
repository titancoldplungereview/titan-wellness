Titan Cold Plunge FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED

How cold does a Titan Cold Plunge get?
The Titan Cold Plunge Arctic Series 100 cools to 39°F (4°C), while the Arctic Pro Series reaches 37°F (3°C). Our Elite Chiller can drop water temperature to as low as 35°F for commercial applications. All models allow you to set any target temperature via the digital control panel or the Titan Wellness app.
What makes Titan Cold Plunge different from competitors?
Titan Cold Plunge systems offer significantly more customization than competing brands at the same price point. This includes Wi-Fi app control with scheduling, dual UV + ozone sanitation, custom shell colors, and a 3-year comprehensive warranty that covers parts, compressor, and labor. Most competitors offer only basic digital controls with no app integration and shorter warranties.
Does Titan Wellness offer financing on cold plunges?
Yes — Titan Wellness offers 0% APR financing on all Titan Cold Plunge systems. The Arctic Series 100 starts at $69/month, the Arctic Pro at $91/month, and the Elite Chiller at $136/month. Approval takes just minutes and there are no hidden fees or prepayment penalties.
How do I maintain a Titan Cold Plunge ice bath?
Titan Cold Plunge systems are designed for low maintenance. The built-in UV and ozone dual sanitation (Pro Series and above) keeps water clean for 4–6 weeks before needing a water change. For all models, we recommend a quick filter rinse monthly and a water change every 4–8 weeks depending on usage. The Titan app sends maintenance reminders automatically.
Can a Titan Water Chiller be used outdoors?
All Titan Cold Plunge tubs and Titan Water Chillers are rated for indoor and outdoor use. The chillers carry an IP55 weather-resistance rating, suitable for covered patios and open-air installations. For climates with freezing winters, we recommend the insulated housing upgrade or moving the chiller unit indoors during extreme cold snaps.
